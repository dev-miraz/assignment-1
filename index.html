Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Bangladesh has made significant strides in technological innovation, fostering a dynamic ecosystem that supports startups and embraces digital advancements.

Sutainability

Bangladesh is actively pursuing sustainability through various initiatives aimed at environmental conservation, climate resilience, and green growth.

Infrastructure

Bangladesh is actively advancing its infrastructure to support economic growth and improve living standards.

Empowerment

Bangladesh has made significant strides in empowering its citizens, particularly women and youth, through various initiatives aimed at enhancing skills.

The Vision of Futures


Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.

Zero Poverty: Eliminating poverty through innovative financial solutions like microcredit, enabling individuals to access capital and improve their livelihoods. Zero Unemployment: Promoting social businesses that create employment opportunities, particularly for marginalized communities, fostering economic inclusion. Zero Net Carbon Emissions: Encouraging sustainable practices and green technologies to address environmental challenges and combat climate change.
